About

David Hill better known as Young Ess is an upcoming UK rapper from South London. He began making music in his early teens after being heavily influenced by the likes of Giggs, Nipsey Hussle, Jay-Z and Chronixx. He has released his official debut album ‘Stay Down Till You Come Up’ 2 nd Feb. The album highlights his return to music as a conscious rapper with a lot of knowledge and life experiences to share, through his unique narrative in his music. Expect plenty of buzz around Young Ess this year from live shows to future releases.
